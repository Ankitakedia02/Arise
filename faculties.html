Samartha Deshmukh

Founder

Samartha is one of the founder of Arise. He is a businessman who owns 1 company named Lana Valves Manufacturing. His company has to its credentials the G.S. Parkhe Award by MCCI Pune for the Best Product of the Year. Earlier he was a student where he learnt guitar but afterwards his interest in the field of music drove him towards collaboarting with Pranay and opening a friendly academy in 2018.

Pranay Patil

Founder & Guitarist

Pranay is one of the founders of Arise and a guitarist by profession. He is a Civil Engineer but his passion towards music lead him to open a Music and Art Academy where he can distribute his knowledge make a thousand of lives happier. Pranay has his own style of teaching music starting with basics to expert level. He believes learning comes from within and also has a strong belief in ear sense as we all feel music through hearing. Pranay help students to build their ear sense in music.

Swapnil Roy Choudhury

Hindustani Classical Singer

Swapnil is a Hindustani Classical Singer. He has represented Indian Classical music in South Korea and Japan. His achievements in IIT Kharagpur as winner in band competetion, runner up in IIT Bombay and Rajya Sabha Akademy annual competetion in bhajan and Nazrul Geeti & many more. Besides that, he is an IT professional. With all these achievements he is teaching his students to learn sangeet with daily riyaz. His main Mantra is Riyaz.

Ankita Kedia

Visual Artist

Ankita is a self taught Visual Artist. She has started drawing as a kid but later on the interest grew on and so her love for Art. Her passion towards painting and urge to teach children lead her to come forward on a journey with Arise. She teaches sketching, and uses of water colors, acrylic colors, oil colors & much more. Being on a 9 to 5 job she manages to paint and explore.
